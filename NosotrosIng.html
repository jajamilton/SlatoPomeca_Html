About Us

Hacienda Ecoturistica El Salto del Pómeca, is an eco-adventure tourism project. Our location and headquarters allows us to enjoy the beauty and tranquility that nature offers, gives us the opportunity to use these resources to enjoy an adventure, where we will learn to appreciate the simple things that nature offers us, generating relaxing experiences such as admire the landscape, walk along paths, enjoy a bath in the river, admire the waterfall, feel the sensation of fishing, and admire the landscape.

Mission

Satisfy the expectations and needs of our customers and guests, providing them with quality products and services, and friendly with the environment, generating at the same time an ecotourism culture in the region.

View

To be recognized as the leading Ecotourism Hacienda in the province of Ricaurte, being the best option for holding events, and in turn for fun and rest, taking into account sustainable development through environmental processes and maintaining high quality standards in the provision of the service.


Objectives

Teach our clients and guests to appreciate and preserve all that nature gives us, making known the municipalities of Moniquirá and Toguí, landscape and culturally, probing for ecological preservation, so that new generations have and can enjoy an environment sustainable environment and without pollution.

Vocation of services

It is the willingness to serve in an organized, friendly and enthusiastic manner.

Excellence

It is the constant search for the highest degree of quality in all areas and areas of our organization.

Integrity

behaviors in accordance with ethical and moral principles based on honesty, rectitude and fairness.
